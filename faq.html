FAQ

(Frequently asked questions)

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

  1. Open this page
  2. Log in with your data
  3. Upload all the files, which you want to print
  4. Find the printer (it is located on the first floor next to the escalator)
  5. Scan your student card
  6. Choose the printing option and wait for your documents to be printed

If you encounter any problems you can always message HZ to ask for tips, but note that it may take some time to wait for the responce.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Follow the instructions from this page

How can I buy something
(like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Log into your account after clicking this link.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

  1. Book it on this page
  2. Go to the "Make a reservation" section
  3. Follow through by clicking the "Reserve meeting room or Project Group room"
  4. Choose a room

Please book a room for the times you are sure of usage, and do not block the whole calendar (there are other students as well)

What are the instructions if you want to park
your car at the HZ parking lot?

  1. Find the parking lot
  2. Scan your student card at the barriers
  3. Find a good place to park

Please note that there are a lot of students and not that many parking spots, so there can be days during which you'll have to find other place to park.
